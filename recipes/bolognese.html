Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Everyone needs a great everyday Spaghetti Bolognese recipe, and this is mine!

The Bolognese Sauce is rich, thick and has beautiful depth of flavour. Itâ€™s perfect for a quick midweek meal but even better if you can simmer it for a couple of hours!

  • Ground beef
  • Spaghetti
  • Olive Oil
  • Onion
  • Tomato paste
  • Red wine

Steps

  1. Put a pot with water and wait for it to boil
  2. Saute diced onion with olive oil on medium heat
  3. Add beef
  4. Cook beef unlit it's nice and brown
  5. Add seasoning of choice, tomato paste and a little bit of wine
  6. Add spaghetti to boiling water
  7. Cook bolognese for 10 minutes on low heat
  8. Cook spaghetti until it's nice and soft
  9. Serve spaghetti on plate and pour bolognese over it, and enjoy!
