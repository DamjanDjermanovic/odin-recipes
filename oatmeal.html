Oatmeal

Description

There's one thing I buy in jumbo size in my house it's old fashioned oats!

I share my bowl of oatmeal often on Instagram and Snapchat and I always get questions about how to make oatmeal.

  • Rolled oats
  • Cookies of choice
  • Protein powder
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Blend the rolled oats with cookies of choice
  2. Put the oats and cookies blend to bowl
  3. Add scoop of protein powder
  4. Pour milk and simultaneously mix with spoon until it gets to a desired consistency
  5. Add garnish like a fancy fitness influencer
  6. Enjoy!
